About Flaburst

Flaburst aims to bring a new level of vaping experience with their rich and flavorful products that comes in 60 ml bottles. Made from the Philippines, Flaburst adheres to its local safety and health policies which assures all products are mixed in our facility, and e-juices are tested by and MSDS certified by a licensed chemist. The team assures not just the unique taste but also the safety of consumers in every bottle. Quality ejuices suit every consumer's need while keeping the VG-PG ratio, Nicotine level, and flavor content in mind. With this, Flaburst came up with these premium e-juices: Icy Dew, Milky Snow and Straw Belt.
